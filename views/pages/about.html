About



Pocket Universe it's a little app that provides brief information (like the mass and size) of some objects of the known universe.

The information showed on this page comes from external sources like NASA web site or even sites like Wikipedia, however I'll try to keep the information up to date as much as possible.

For now, the app status is Under Construction. Once finished you can provide feedback and info to make this app better!
